Proposal for Light in the Forest

This site contains the proposal for 2025 Light in the Forest for the father/son team of Herm and Aaron Greider. This collaborative project blends reclaimed windows and technology to create an engaging light and sound experience. Building on their shared interest for technology, environmental art, and interactive installations, Aaron and Herm will invite audiences on a journey through light and sound.

Step up to the Portals.

As you walk the nature path, soft chimes and flickering forest specters respond to your movements, creating a surreal, immersive experience. The Light Portals use repetition, sound, and subtle interactivity to make visitors feel surrounded by living light, blending the natural and the otherworldly to make the Portals feel malleable and alive.
